Smaph Bootcamps

Each module is taught via a series of weekend intensive (10hr) bootcamps, between which mentees will have daily contact with their mentor, to ensure the new skills and concepts are being integrated into their daily lives.

We structure our modules by bookending the weekdays with two weekend bootcamps. We start with a 10-hr Sunday bootcamp, followed by Monday-Friday distance learning (with solo missions and mentorial videocalls), before another 10-hr Saturday bootcamp. This constitutes one module: two 10-hr bootcamps and 5 days of distance learning inbetween.

To allow maximum attention for each student, bootcamps have 3-6 students per mentor, who are all vetted Masters graduates or PhDs with demonstrable life experience. This ratio allows us to conduct activities that would be impossible in conventional educational circumstances, and allows mentors and mentees to quickly build a real relationship through the challenges and trials of the bootcamp.

Bootcamps take place across a variety of curated locations and involve a holistic blend of practical/interpersonal/intellectual activities, competitive/cooperative games, solo/group projects, demos, debates, workshops, roleplays and more. Our activities and experiences include things such as:

  • ice baths
  • meeting inspiring people
  • live debates
  • sparring matches
  • building things
  • cooking/eating together
  • guided meditation

Whether you're improving physical, mental or social fitness, every bootcamp climaxes with a challenging 'real life experience' in which the mentees must use their new skills in a way that has a real impact on the world, with real consequences, real emotions, and real memories.
