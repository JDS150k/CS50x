Signs of High Smaph

Minimal screen time, exceptional diplomacy, confidence and calmness, good posture, self-motivated, a surplus of mental energy, strong eye contact, a respectable physical presence, emotional intelligence, ample focus, positivity, engaged in the real world, nurturing relationships, holding a true picture of reality, realistic thinking, strong sense of priorities, improving fitness, mentally tough, growing number of “true” friends.

Signs of Low Smaph

Excessive screen time, poor social skills, anxiety or awkwardness, bad posture, lack of self-drive, lethargy, puffy/dark eyes, fragile bodies, bad emotional development, lack of focus, depression, ignoring real life, neglecting relationships, holding a distorted view of reality, unrealistic thoughts, irrational beheaviour, badly chosen priorities, declining fitness, increasing mental fragility, and decreasing number of “true” friends.

How do your bootcamps work?

Each module is taught via a series of weekend intensive (10hr) bootcamps, between which mentees will have daily contact with their mentor, to ensure the new skills and concepts are being integrated into their daily lives. For more information, see our bootcamps page .

Who are your Mentors?

To allow maximum attention for each student, bootcamps have 2-5 students per mentor, who are all vetted Masters graduates or PhDs with demonstrable life experience. Every mentor is an inspiring role model for social, mental, and physical fitness, and has the ability to connect with their mentees. To find out more, see our mentors page .

What are your specialist teaching methods?

We view the chair, desk, and screen as enemies of youth development. Our modules are delivered via exercises, projects and missions which all take place in the real world, in a variety of curated locations, during intensive weekend bootcamps. To find out more, see our teaching method page .

Can you explain the theory and concepts behind smaph?

We combined three robust and battle-tested sociological models to form the theoretical basis for our Smaph services; The Health Triangle, The Fitness Pyramid, and The Pathogenesis/Salutogenesis Dichotomy. To find out more about these theoretical concepts see our theory page .

What modern research supports smaph theory?

Being passionate about youth development means that we keep up to date with all the latest research. Some of the most important youth development studies of recent years directly support what we fight to achieve with smaph. See them all on our dedicated research page .

Who is eligibile to join your programs?

Not everyone is suitable for our smaph bootcamps. To preserve the atmosphere that allows us to guarantee results, we must screen all applicants. This allows us to keep our promise of guaranteed results. You don’t need academic brilliance, sports accolades, or social mastery to join us, but you do have to be an APE in those three areas. To find out more, see our eligibility page .
