Smaph Eligibility

Unfortunately not everyone is suitable for our smaph bootcamps.

To preserve the atmosphere that allows us to guarantee results, we must screen all applicants. This allows us to keep our promise of guaranteed results.

Our Guarantee

We promise that all youths who complete our bootcamps will achieve the learning outcomes listed in the module descriptors, or else they can retake the module for free until they do.

APE Screening Interview

You don't need academic brilliance, sports accolades, or social mastery to join us, but you do have to be an APE in those three areas.

APE stands for:

Ambition – The applicant must WANT to improve theirself, not just be forced to by their parents or teachers. If our mentors don’t sense internal motivation, or cannot rouse it in the interview, then we cannot admit them to our bootcamps.

Potential – The applicant must HAVE the room to improve, which most youths have. Older students may already have some of our taught skills, so we might reject you from those bootcamps because we cannot guarantee your improvement.

Energy – The applicant must DO the things to improve, which requires ample energy. Our bootcamps are extremely demanding. If students have too many other extracurricular commitments, they won’t be able to endure our bootcamps.

You can request a 1-to-1 meeting with our mentors will determine if you have what it takes to join our bootcamps, or if you are not yet ready – in which case we can advise you on how to become prepared for the next time.

Other Requirements:

Bootcamps are delivered in EL, therefore good English Language is required to get the full benefit.

Applicants must be available during the bootcamps. We structure our modules by bookending the weekdays with two weekend bootcamps. We start with a 10-hr Sunday bootcamp, followed by Monday-Friday distance learning (with solo missions and mentorial videocalls), before another 10-hr Saturday bootcamp. See more on our bootcamps page.

We also have zero-tolerance policy on disruptive behaviour, and we will kick you out without warning if you do anything to hinder the bootcamp. This is vital so we can keep our promise of achieving the learning outcomes for all mentees.

We treat all mentees like adults, and we expect respect.

If you think you’re eligible, get in touch via our contact page.
